ARM Microcontroller Frameworks

Here are some firmware development frameworks for certain 32-bit ARM (Advanced RISC Machine) single chip microcontrollers.

I am interested in alternatives to the traditional GCC development environment, so here are some experiments with Astrobe Oberon, Free Pascal, GNAT Ada, Mbed OS, and Mikropascal for ARM microcontrollers.
